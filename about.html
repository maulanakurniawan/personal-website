About Maulana Kurniawan

I’m a software developer based in Indonesia with around 19+ years of experience building, shipping, and maintaining web applications. I still work hands-on every day: writing code, improving architecture, fixing production issues, and turning product ideas into useful software.

My foundation is in PHP and web application development, and Laravel has been a major part of my toolkit for years. But my work is broader than a single framework. I routinely handle system design, API integrations, payment workflows, database modeling, deployment pipelines, and ongoing operational maintenance.

I enjoy building practical tools and refining products over time. My focus is not hype. It’s clarity, reliability, and solving the right technical problem with the right level of complexity.

Skills and areas of work

Application Development

PHP and Laravel development, modular codebases, dashboard/admin systems, and sustainable web application architecture for long-term maintainability.

Product Engineering

SaaS product development from concept to production, including pricing logic, subscription flows, payment integrations, and product UX decisions.

Systems and Operations

Server deployment, monitoring and alerting, webhook pipelines, incident debugging, and practical fixes for real production bottlenecks.

How I work

I prefer clear communication, realistic scope, and iterative delivery. I try to reduce surprises by mapping technical risks early, making trade-offs explicit, and shipping in small dependable steps. Whether I’m working independently or with a team, I value calm execution: solve the problem, validate the result, and leave the system in better shape than before.

Broader technical interests

Beyond core development work, I spend time on automation, technical SEO foundations for product sites, integration reliability, and the practical side of operating software in the real world. I’m also interested in the maker side of tech: experimenting, launching small tools, learning from users, and iterating with discipline.
